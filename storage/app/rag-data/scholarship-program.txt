Paarl Secondary Academy offers a limited number of scholarships to academically talented students.

Scholarships are awarded based on academic achievement, leadership potential, community involvement, and financial need.

Applicants must submit academic records from the previous two years and complete a scholarship application form.

Selected students may be invited to attend an interview.

Scholarships may cover between 25 percent and 100 percent of tuition fees depending on individual circumstances.

Scholarship applications close on September 30 each year.
