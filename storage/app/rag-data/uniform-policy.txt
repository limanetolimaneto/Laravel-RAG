Students are required to wear the official school uniform during all academic activities.

The standard uniform consists of a school blazer, white shirt, grey trousers or skirt, school tie, and black shoes.

Sports uniforms must be worn during physical education classes and official sporting events.

Uniform items must be clean, properly fitted, and in good condition.

Jewelry should be kept to a minimum and must not interfere with safety or learning activities.

Students who fail to comply with the uniform policy may be subject to disciplinary action.
