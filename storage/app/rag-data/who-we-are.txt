Paarl Secondary Academy is a private secondary school located in Paarl, Western Cape, South Africa.

The school serves students from Grade 8 to Grade 12 and focuses on academic excellence, personal development, and community engagement.

Our mission is to provide a safe and supportive learning environment where students can develop critical thinking skills, leadership abilities, and a lifelong passion for learning.

The academy offers a balanced curriculum that combines traditional academic subjects with sports, arts, and extracurricular activities.

Paarl Secondary Academy welcomes students from diverse backgrounds and promotes respect, responsibility, and integrity in all aspects of school life.
