The annual tuition fee for the 2026 academic year is R48,000 per student.

Families may choose one of the following payment options:

Annual payment before January 31: R46,000.

Quarterly payments: Four installments of R12,500.

Monthly payments: Twelve installments of R4,200.

A registration fee of R1,500 is payable upon acceptance of admission.

Tuition fees cover classroom instruction, access to school facilities, and participation in standard academic programs.

Additional costs may apply for uniforms, field trips, sports tournaments, and special extracurricular activities.
