The school cafeteria provides nutritious meals and refreshments for students and staff.

Breakfast is available from 07:00 to 08:00.

Lunch is served from 12:30 to 14:00.

The menu includes sandwiches, salads, hot meals, fruit, and vegetarian options.

Students are encouraged to make healthy food choices and remain hydrated throughout the school day.

Cashless payment methods are accepted at all cafeteria service points.
